The Recipe For Disaster (OFFICIAL) tm


description:

with outstanding results and a 100% success rate, this recipe is perfect to spice up any boring night in. the more committed you are, the better the results

Author: Drew Mahl

Prep Time: 0 minutes

Yield: Serves a Lifetime of Regret for 3



FEEDBACK SHEET

Ingredients

  • 3 (or more) bored girls

  • 0 plans

  • 110% commitment to the bit

  • $10 (preferably in coins)

  • the brainpower required to draft 1 vaguely worded request implying that you want your neighbors to vacate their residence

  • 1 set of mystery neighbors

  • absolutely NO awareness



    • Instructions

  • concieve a plan: this part is very simple, all it takes is a few seconds to think up one (1) way to mess with the neighbors down the hall.

  • gather support: float your ideas to the group and see which one gains the most traction.

  • collect materials: draft your note and enclose the $10.

  • start to convince yourself this is a good idea: positive affirmations such as "nothing bad could possibly come of this" and "this is normal behavior" are good places to start.

  • make the move: go slip that note under your mystery neightbors' door!

  • wait: this is the hardest part. the ball is in your neighbors court now, so sit back, relax, and await the impending mayhem.

  • play it by ear (PIBE): when you get your response, PIBE! just remember to stay 110% committed to the bit.



    • if you follow these directions exactly, you will have a foolproof map to an exciting night, and enough embarassment for a lifetime <3

    Notes


  • no two recipes yield the exact same responses, but you are guaranteed a fun night. Share your unique experiences with others below!
